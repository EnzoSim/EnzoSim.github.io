## Processing notes

- The Stripe Press asset is the publisher's full print texture. The front-cover panel was cropped from that texture before WebP conversion.
- The two Penguin Random House endpoints currently return 450-pixel-tall official jackets. They were preserved at source resolution rather than upscaled.
- The Simon & Schuster and Yale jackets were downscaled to 1200 pixels tall to keep the reading page light while retaining more than enough detail for the rendered shelf.
